Ugandan Applicant Requirements

To apply from Uganda, you must meet the following criteria:

  • Must be a Ugandan citizen
  • Aged 20–30 years
  • Completed secondary education in Uganda
  • Completed at least 2 years of university studies
  • Teaching or volunteering experience is an advantage
  • Minimum B1 level in French (DELF/DALF required)
  • Must be a native English speaker
  • Preferably single
  • Must show proof of funds for flight and first month's expenses
