Application Process

Application Timeline

This is the general timeline — exact dates vary by country, so always confirm with your local French Embassy, Alliance Française, or Institut Français.

Nov – Mar

Applications Open

The application window opens, though exact dates vary by country.

Feb – Apr

Interviews & Evaluations

Shortlisted applicants go through interviews and evaluations.

Apr – Jul

Results

Applicants are notified of the outcome of their application.

Sept

Arrival in France

Successful assistants travel to France ahead of the October start of the school year.

Tip: Preparation determines how successful your experience will be. Start gathering your documents and researching your academy early — don't wait for the results to start preparing.
